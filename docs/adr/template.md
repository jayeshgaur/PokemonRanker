# D-N — Title

> Template for new architectural decisions. To add a decision: copy this file, name it `D-N-short-slug.md`, fill it in, and append a corresponding entry to `docs/DECISIONS.md`. Decisions are immutable once locked; to change one, write a new ADR that supersedes it.

## Decision

A single, declarative statement of what we are committing to. Avoid hedging. If you cannot phrase it as one sentence, you may not yet have a decision.

## Why

The reasoning. What pressure prompted this decision? What constraint or opportunity does it address? Future-you should be able to read this and judge whether the pressure still applies.

## Rejected alternatives

Bulleted list of what we considered and turned down, each with one line of reasoning. Decisions are interesting because of what they exclude; documenting the rejections is half the value.

- *Alternative 1.* Why it was rejected.
- *Alternative 2.* Why it was rejected.

## Reversibility

Low / Medium / High cost to undo. Lock confidently when reversibility is high cost; revisit happily when it's low.

## Consequences

What downstream work this decision implies, enables, or rules out. Often the place to surface things future phases will need to budget for.
